Instructions

Welcome to the Game! Here are the instructions to get started:

  1. Step 1: Press the START button. Simon will give the first colour signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color..

  2. Step 2: Simon will duplicate the first signal and add one. Repeat these two signals by pressing the same color buttons, in order.

  3. Step 3: Simon will duplicate these first two signals and add one.

  4. Step 4: Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.

  5. Step 5: If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, Simon responds with a "RAZZ" sound. This means you've lost, and the sequence of signals ends.

If you have any questions, please contact me.
